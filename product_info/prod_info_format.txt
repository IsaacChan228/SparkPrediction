	profInfo.csv data format:
	
	id: unique numeric identifier for each row
	parent_prod_id: Parent ID of the product (category of the product) (a_yyyy, where yyyy is a 16-digit combination of letters and numbers)
	main_category: the category of the product
	price: the price of the product
	title: name of the product
	features: description of the product
	store: the store selling the product
	rating_number: number of ratings received for this product (non-negative integer)
